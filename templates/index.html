med mappr


The product marketplace is consistently in flux. med mappr helps you stay ahead of competition by helping you accelerate of early product adoption and identify engagement opportunities

Find a physician's prescribing DNA

Understanding the best way to utilize your sales force is crucial to maintaining market stature. With publicly-available data, med mappr can profile a physician's prescribing DNA and analyzes the drug marketplace to identify the best touchpoints and opportunities for your sales force to engage with physicians.

We seek to answer some of these questions:

  • How much are competitors already reaching this physician?
  • How readily does a physician adopt new drugs in place of more established ones?
  • Determine how demographics influence prescribing behavior

Analysis of Oncology Prescribing Behavior

This project focuses on predicting top-prescribing physicians for oncology drugs. Using Imbruvica, an oral inhibitor of Bruton's tyrosine kinase for treatment of mantel cell lymphoma and chronic lymphocytic leukemia, I analyzed Medicare claims data to build a gradient boosted classifier model that predicts whether a physician will be a top prescriber in the following year.
